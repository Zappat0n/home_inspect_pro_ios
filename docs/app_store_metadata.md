## Description (English)

Home Inspect Pro transforms your iPhone into a complete home inspection toolkit. Designed for professional home inspectors, this app streamlines the entire inspection workflow — from checklists and photos to professional PDF reports.

### Key Features

**Smart Checklists**
Country-specific inspection templates with 60+ items per template. Mark items as OK, Defect, or N/A with one tap. Templates available for USA (InterNACHI / ASHI Standard), Canada (CAHPI Standard), and Spain (ITE / IEE Standard). Add comments to any item.

**Photo Evidence**
Capture and attach unlimited photos to any checklist item. Built-in camera integration for instant capture. Works offline — photos queue and sync when you reconnect.

**Digital Signatures**
Collect client signatures directly on screen. Clean, professional signature capture with PencilKit. Signature appears automatically on PDF reports.

**Professional PDF Reports**
Auto-generate branded inspection reports with one tap. Reports include property details, checklist results, defect summary, photos, and client signature. Email reports directly to clients from the app.

**Offline Mode**
Complete entire inspections without internet access. All data syncs automatically when you reconnect. Perfect for properties with limited connectivity.

**Templates for Every Market**
- United States: InterNACHI / ASHI Standard (80+ items across 10 categories)
- Canada: CAHPI Standard (67 items across 11 categories)
- Spain: ITE / IEE Standard (58 items across 9 categories)

### Subscription
